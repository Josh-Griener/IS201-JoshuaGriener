- My Family -


 Both of my parents are from here in Utah. They met in highschool while living in the Salt Lake City area, studied at BYU together, and got married in 1984!

 Like I previously mentioned I am the youngest of 6! I have 3 older brothers and 2 older sisters.

  • Jared is the oldest and lives in California. He works in solar sales and loves to play poker!
  • Sarah is next and she lives here in Utah. She has a degree in marketing from UVU and works for the Utah Jazz.
  • My next brother is Stephen, who passed away from cancer when he was in his 20s. He loved sports and was studying to become a physical therapist.

  • My last sibling before me is Nathan, who loves music and playing the guitar. He lives in Salt Lake as well, studied archeology at Utah state and is currently applying for different graduate programs.

 I love my siblings! we all have different personalities, hobbies, and lifestyles but we all get along great and love hanging out together.
